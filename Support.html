Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How does the AI generate bike suggestions? The AI generates bike suggestions based on the information you provide, such as your height, weight, preferred riding style, and budget. It takes into account your responses to questions about your experience level, desired features, and riding conditions to make personalized recommendations.

2. Can I trust the AI's suggestions? Our AI is designed to provide high-quality, personalized recommendations based on your input. However, it is important to keep in mind that no AI system is perfect and there is always a chance that the recommendations may not be exactly what you are looking for.

3. Can I customize my search results? Yes, you can use the filters on our website to customize your search results based on factors such as price, brand, or specific features. You can also adjust your responses to our questions to get more tailored suggestions.

4. How do I know if a bike is in stock? Our website provides real-time inventory information for each bike model. If a bike is out of stock, you can sign up to receive an email notification when it becomes available again.

5. What is your return policy? We have a 30-day return policy for unused bikes, subject to certain conditions. Please see our Warranty & Returns policy for more information.

6. How long does it take to receive my bike after I order it? Delivery times vary depending on your location, shipping method, and the availability of the bike you ordered. In general, we strive to get your bike to you as quickly as possible while ensuring safe and reliable delivery.

7. What if I have additional questions or need help selecting a bike? Our customer service team is available to help you with any questions or concerns you may have. You can contact us by phone, email, or live chat during business hours. We also have a bike expert available to help you select the perfect bike for your needs.

Warranty and Returns

Warranty: Our company offers a limited warranty on all bicycles purchased through our website. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship for a period of one year from the date of purchase. The warranty is valid only for the original purchaser and is not transferable.

If your bike is found to have a defect covered by the warranty, we will repair or replace the bike, at our discretion, free of charge. To make a claim under the warranty, you must contact our customer service department and provide proof of purchase.

The warranty does not cover normal wear and tear, damage caused by misuse or abuse, or damage caused by improper assembly or maintenance. We reserve the right to determine what constitutes a defect in materials or workmanship.

Returns:We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase from our website. If you are not satisfied, you may return the item within 30 days of the purchase date for a refund or exchange. The item must be in its original, unused condition and in its original packaging.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service department. We will provide you with a return authorization number and instructions for returning the item.

The customer is responsible for all shipping costs associated with returns and exchanges, unless the item was received in a damaged or defective condition. Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment.

Please note that custom bicycles or bicycles with custom modifications are not eligible for returns or exchanges.
