About Us

Description

About the business:

This Bike Shop aims to provide the best and most suitable bike for every customer. Where we try to understand our customer to a deeper level, to identify their goals, needs, wants, preferences, style and current physical state. With the help of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that will automatically address any questions, provide wide range of suggestions that is suitable for customer. It is a one-stop-shop for all your cycling needs, offering a wide range of products, services, knowledge, automation and a welcoming and supportive community.

Goals

Future plans:

My goal is to establish the best bike shop that consists a wide range selection of bikes for different riders including road bikes, mountain bikes, cruiser bikes, hybrid bikes, commuter bikes, BMX bikes, folding bikes, tandem bikes and e-bikes. Together with high-quality accessories and gear including helmets, lights, locks, forks, pumps, tires, brake pads, clothing, lubricants, cleaning materials, and tools. Furthermore, a bike shop that offers professional repair and maintenance services like a skilled mechanic who can perform repairs and maintenance on all types of bikes. That can fix everything from flat tires to more complex issues. A bike shop that organizes events, rides, and supporting local cycling teams or clubs. A welcoming community of riders that give advice and support on all things regarding cycling. On top of that a bike shop that has a great online presence that provide online sales, service, and support to customers.


I’m also planning to develop an Artificial Intelligence (AI) to generate fast and accurate response for customer. A website that asks customers in a form to describe what type of bike do they want, things like are the roads in your area rocky, paved or flat? Do you want a bike build for speed, for calm rides, or for commute? I’m doing this to understand my customers better to make them feel that we care about them and provide them a nice experience just by buying a bike from me. After they answer the form, I will have an AI to make certain combination of parts of the bike they describe then customize it and recommend 6-10 or more types relative to what they describe in the form so, they will have plenty of options. Where every bike they click will have an explanation of its perks, it’s capability and more information about the bike (with an option of text to speech if they prefer listening than reading). Customers will be able to see different parts of the bike that can be replaceable for them to customize it to their liking. Before proceeding to payment and delivery fee.

Business Idea Orgin

I realized after pandemic lots of people are starting to take care of their body or health because people realized that “health is wealth” in order to survive longer we must be healthy. Imagine you study hard most of your life constantly sleep deprived, physically inactive due to obsession of high grades, and everyday living a sedentary life. In the future even though you achieve high grades, become a valedictorian or something your life expectancy surely deteriorated cause of your unhealthy life style, making you susceptible for more diseases, and dying earlier. That’s why I realized being physically active is important, to develop a healthy body that will let you live longer and experience more things in life. I had this thought since pandemic and I know I’m not the only one because I notice many people around me or even in the internet began taking care of themselves, and the demand for bikes increased because they want to be more physically active to develop a healthy body.

Mission

I myself got obsessed with bikes at a certain point, where I learned different types of bikes like cross-road bike, gravel bike, mountain bike, road bike, fat bike etc. even their parts and materials that are good for bikes to last longer and provide better biking experience, including how to maintain or clean the bike and its parts. So, in the future I want to establish a bike shop where I will share all the cool stuff I know about biking, make more people be interested and excited about it, for them to be physically active, to develop a healthy habit and enjoy life more.
